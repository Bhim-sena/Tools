How to Compress Images

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = better quality)
  3. Select your desired output format (or keep original)
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  5. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster websites: Optimized images load quicker, improving user experience
  • Better SEO: Search engines favor fast-loading pages
  • Reduced storage: Save space on your devices and servers
  • Faster sharing: Smaller files transfer quicker via email or messaging

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality.

Supported Formats

  • JPG/JPEG: Best for photographs and complex images with many colors
  • PNG: Ideal for graphics with transparency or sharp edges
  • WEBP: Modern format offering superior compression (25-35% smaller than JPG)
